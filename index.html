About Electric Cars

An electric car is a car powered by one or more electric motors with energy from a battery. Electric cars have been around for ninety one years the first one being invented in 1828 by a man by the name of Anyos Jedlik. This early electric car consisted of only an electric motor. The first modern car was invented by Toyota. electric cars have just become popular because of advances in the battery and desires to reduse green house gas emissions. electric cars are also easily charged, the charging stations can be instaled at home, work, or you can find them at some gas stations.
